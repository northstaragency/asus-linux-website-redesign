Legal & Privacy

Version 1.2 • Effective Date: October 1, 2025 • Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Privacy Policy

ROG Control Center is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Key Definitions

Personal Data
Any information that can identify you as an individual, such as email addresses or IP addresses.
Analytics
Anonymous usage statistics that help us improve the software, such as feature adoption rates or error frequencies.
Telemetry
Technical data about your system and how the application performs, sent with your consent.

What We Collect

ROG Control Center collects minimal data to function and improve:

  • No personal data is collected by the application itself
  • Optional telemetry (hardware model, feature usage, crash reports) only if you explicitly opt in
  • Website analytics use privacy-respecting services (no third-party trackers)
  • No tracking across sites or selling of any data

Important: The application runs entirely on your device. No personal information is required to use ROG Control Center.

Data Collection Details

Application Usage

When you use ROG Control Center:

  • All settings are stored locally on your device
  • No account or registration is required
  • Hardware control happens directly via system daemons—no cloud services

Optional Telemetry

If you enable telemetry (opt-in only):

  • Hardware model to improve device support
  • Feature usage to prioritize development
  • Crash reports with stack traces (no personal data)
  • Performance metrics to identify bottlenecks

You can disable telemetry at any time in Settings → Privacy.

Website Analytics

Our website uses privacy-respecting analytics:

  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • Anonymized page views and referrers
  • No personally identifiable information
  • Compliant with GDPR and CCPA

Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  1. Access your data — Request a copy of any data we hold
  2. Correct your data — Update or fix inaccurate information
  3. Delete your data — Request complete removal from our systems
  4. Opt out — Disable telemetry or analytics at any time
  5. Data portability — Export your settings and preferences
  6. Object to processing — Refuse certain data uses

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@asus-linux.org. We will respond within 30 days.

No data retention: Since most data is stored locally, uninstalling the application removes all associated data from your device.
